Chicken

Chicken is one of the most widely consumed meats globally, known for its versatility, nutritional value, and relatively mild flavor. It can be prepared in numerous ways, making it a staple in various cuisines and an integral part of many traditional and modern dishes.Chicken is a rich source of high-quality protein, essential for muscle growth and repair. It is also a good source of vitamins and minerals such as vitamin B6, niacin (vitamin B3), phosphorus, and selenium. Compared to red meats, chicken typically has lower fat content, especially when the skin is removed, making it a healthier choice for those watching their fat intake.Breast: Lean and versatile, often used in grilling, baking, and sautéing. Thighs and Drumsticks: Juicier and more flavorful, suitable for slow cooking, braising, and frying. Wings: Popular in appetizers, often fried or grilled and served with various sauces.
